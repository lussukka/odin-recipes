Catfood

Very delicious food. Easy and cheap to made. Kids like it.
You only need a frying pan, pot and some time.

Ingredients

  • 3 Tuna cans
  • Handfull of spaghetti
  • Half pot of water
  • 1dl Cheese cream
  • A pinch of pepper
  • A Little bit salt

Steps

  1. Put the pot on the stove. Fill the pot with half of water and put the spaghetti in.
  2. Heat the tuna in a frying pan.
  3. When the tuna is almost cooked, add salt and pepper.
  4. Add the cream to the tuna and stir until the cream thickens.
  5. When spaghetti is coocked remove water and add the tuna to pot.
  6. Mix well and serve.
  7. Eat with happy smile on your face.
