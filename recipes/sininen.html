Sininen

Poor man food for whole family. You only need oven pan, knife and hot oven.

Ingredients

  • Hk sininen, but if you can't buy it use cheapest sausages what you get.(Look picture above.)
  • Some cheese
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard

Steps

  1. Put the sausages in an ovenproof dish and make a longitudinal incision in them
  2. Add plenty of ketchup and mustard to the incisions
  3. Add cheese above sausages
  4. Put in to hot oven. (~200c) and let it be there 20 min.
  5. Enjoy with bottle of beer.
