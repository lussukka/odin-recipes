Tutti Ti Macaroni

2nd easiest food in the world. You will only need frying pan and pot.

Ingredients

  • 3dl Macaroni
  • 400g Minced meat
  • 2dl Cream
  • A little salt

Steps

  1. Put Macaroni in the pot and add half litre of water. Let them boil until ready.
  2. Cook minced meat on frying pan.
  3. Pour off excess water and mix meat and macaroni.
  4. Add cream and boil until it looks good.
  5. Add some salt.
  6. Serve and enjoy the meal.
